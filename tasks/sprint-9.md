# Sprint 9

Goal

Perform a comprehensive localization sweep to eliminate hardcoded English text in all core UI screens and fully support both English (EN) and Bahasa Indonesia (ID) through the Settings Language Switcher.

---

Epic

Localization & i18n Sweep

---

Task 1

Update translation JSON dictionaries (`en.json`, `id.json`) to include settings, home, history, and outcome keys.

---

Task 2

Unlock the language selection switcher buttons in the Settings screen and translate all settings texts.

---

Task 3

Translate the Home Screen's board details (tickets, category tags, CTAs, and dynamic race titles).

---

Task 4

Translate the History Screen and Result Screen texts (such as dynamic share cards, headers, and buttons).

---

Definition of Done

Language selection in settings switches active translations instantly.

Both English (EN) and Bahasa Indonesia (ID) translate all screens seamlessly without fallback errors.

Zero hardcoded user-facing strings are present in Home, Settings, History, or Result screens.

All biome checks, typescript builds, and vitest runs are successful.
