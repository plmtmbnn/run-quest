# ADR-001

Title

No Backend for Phase 0

---

Status

Accepted

---

Decision

Phase 0 will not use a backend.

---

Context

The objective is validating gameplay.

Infrastructure would slow iteration.

---

Consequences

Pros

Very fast iteration.

Offline support.

Zero infrastructure cost.

Simple deployment.

Cons

No cloud save.

No leaderboard.

No analytics.

---

Future

Backend may be introduced in Phase 1 without changing the Simulation Engine.
